ABOUT BLDEA

BLDEA (Bijapur Lingayat Education Association) is a major educational organization in North Karnataka with 75+ institutions spanning professional colleges, schools, and research centers.
Its mission focuses on:

Social and economic transformation through education

Empowering women and underprivileged groups

Reducing inequality in educational access

Promoting academic excellence and research

HISTORY OF BLDEA
Inception (1910–1914)

Founded on 23 October 1910 by Vachana Pitamaha Dr. P. G. Halakatti, a noted literary figure and social reformer.

In 1914, BLDEA took over the New English School, later renamed Siddeshwara Secondary Education School.

Expansion Period (1942–1960s)

Led by Shri Shri Sanganabasaveshwar Maha Shivayogigalu of Banthanal.

Established multiple primary schools, high schools, and colleges.

Professional Education Era (1960s)

Shri B. M. Patil expanded BLDEA into professional education.

Institutions founded: Engineering, Pharmacy, Nursing, Medical Colleges, and a 1,000-bed teaching hospital.

Modern Era (1990s–Today)

Dr. M. B. Patil (General Secretary) modernized BLDEA.

Added multiple courses and worked toward the creation of a university.

BLDE University was established on 29 February 2008.

Regional Development

BLDEA serves socially and economically backward regions in Vijayapura and Bagalkot.
It offers inclusive education with no discrimination based on caste, sect, religion, or creed.
